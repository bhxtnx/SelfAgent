## 📘 What is SelfAgent?

SelfAgent is an open-source AI personal assistant that runs directly on your computer. It lets you control your AI tools without sending your data to external servers. You choose how the AI works, what it can access, and when. This means you get privacy, control, and automation all in one place.

SelfAgent supports popular AI models like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more. It works on multiple platforms, using Python under the hood to help you automate tasks, answer questions, and manage your digital life with ease.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

Before you start, make sure your computer meets these basic needs:

- Operating System: Windows 10 or later, macOS 10.15 or later, or Linux (Ubuntu 18.04+ recommended)
- Processor: At least a 2 GHz dual-core processor
- RAM: Minimum 4 GB (8 GB recommended for best performance)
- Storage: At least 500 MB of free space for installation
- Internet connection: Required for downloading and connecting to AI services, but your data stays local

You do not need any special technical skills to run SelfAgent. It comes ready to use once installed.

## 🔧 Basic Setup and Usage

After the first launch, SelfAgent will guide you through a quick setup:

- **Create Your Profile:** Enter your name and preferences to personalize your experience.
- **Connect AI Services:** Choose the AI models you want to use. You can link public APIs like OpenAI or use locally installed models.
- **Privacy Settings:** Decide how and where your data will be stored. Your conversations stay on your device unless you allow otherwise.
- **Automation Preferences:** Set up tasks you want to automate, such as sending emails, managing files, or scheduling reminders.
